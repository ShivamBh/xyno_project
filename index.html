Seamless Calls is a free Voice Over IP (commonly known as VoIP internet telephony) service provider.


Our mission is to make communication as simple and easy as technological advancements allow it to be. We help integrate how you choose to make, receive or manage your calls.


There are two things you are going to need to indulge in our service. The first is an IAX/SIP-Address, which we shall provide to you for no cost at all. You will also need a software to use the service. Any SIP aware software will do, but we recommend the free Zoiper softphone, which has an HD Audio built-in and X-platfrom flexibilities.


So get your mobile and IP Desk Phone Dialer together and register with www.seamlesscalls.com now.
